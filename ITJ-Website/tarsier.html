    About the Philippine Tarsier

    The Philippine Tarsier is a small primate found only in the Philippines. It is critically endangered due to habitat loss and hunting.

    Primary Threats
    Habitat loss
    Hunting
    Agricultural Conversion
    Logging and Firewood Harvesting
    Pet Trade And Tourism Abuse
    Habitat
    Forests of Bohol, Leyte, Samar, and Mindanao
    Population
    Less than 5,000 - 10,000
    Conservation
    • Protection programs
    • Community engagement
    • Research And Monitoring
    • Habitat restoration
