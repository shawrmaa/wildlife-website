    • Isabela oriole

    Oriolus isabellae
    Conservation Status: Critically Endangered
    Population: 40-50
    About the Isabela Oriole

    It is an oriole identified by its yellowish green plumage and a very large gray bill. This species was presumed to be extinct for many years until its rediscovery in December 1993 near Diffun, Quirino, and in Mansarong in September 1994.

    Primary Threats
    Lowland Forest
    Illegal Logging
    Poaching
    Mining
    Habitat
    Luzon
    Population
    It is critically endangered with estimates of just 50 to 250 mature individuals remaining.
